# 📊 E-Commerce Sales Analytics | End-to-End Data Analytics Project

## 📌 Project Overview

This project is an end-to-end Data Analytics solution built using **Excel, SQL Server, and Power BI**. The objective is to analyze sales performance, customer behavior, product performance, profitability, and regional trends to generate actionable business insights.

The project demonstrates the complete analytics workflow from data cleaning to dashboard creation and business recommendations.

---

# 🎯 Business Problem

The company wants to understand:

- Which products generate the highest revenue?
- Which regions contribute the most sales?
- Which customer segments are most valuable?
- Which categories are most profitable?
- How do sales change over time?
- How can business performance be improved?

# 📈 Key Business Insights

1. West region generated the highest sales.
2. Technology is the highest-performing category.
3. Canon products generated the highest sales.
4. Monthly sales show an upward trend.
5. Consumer segment contributes the largest share of sales.
6. Technology is the most profitable category.
7. Some categories have high sales but relatively lower profit, indicating margin improvement opportunities.
8. The top 10 customers contribute a significant share of total revenue.
9. Standard shipping accounts for a large number of orders.
10. Revenue is concentrated among a limited number of products.

---

# 💡 Business Recommendations

- Increase investment in the West region.
- Expand Technology product inventory.
- Maintain sufficient stock for high-selling Canon products.
- Improve profitability of low-margin categories.
- Focus retention campaigns on high-value customers.
- Optimize discount strategies.
- Use seasonal sales trends for inventory planning.
- Promote high-profit products through targeted marketing.
